Need 2 — Use-Case Governance · Step 2 of 3

Pedagogical Context

These questions feed the four Cathedral layers. Answer what you can.

1
The Cornerstone — Values & SAMR Magis Test
Efficiency vs. Magis · Substitution vs. Redefinition
Is this doing "the same thing faster" (Substitution) or "a genuinely new thing better" (Redefinition/Magis)?
→ Does this use serve the Magis, or substitute human effort without formative value?
2
The Scriptorium — Pedagogy & Bloom's / IPP
Cognitive load · Pilot vs. Passenger · Mandatory reflection
Is the student the thinker, or is the AI doing the thinking?
→ Is there a mandatory reflection or Ignatian Examen step built into this use?
3
The Ambo — Doctrine & Truth Standard
Studiousness vs. Sloth · Hallucination risk · Developmental appropriateness
Does this promote the pursuit of truth, or the avoidance of effort?
→ Would a student become more capable or more dependent from this use?
4
The Bell Tower — Transparency
Parental disclosure · Explainability · Radical honesty
If a parent asks "Did AI help my son?" — can you answer clearly and confidently?
→ Is there a clear, honest answer ready for parents, students, and administration?
